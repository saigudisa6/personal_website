My programming journey began in 9th grade when I said Hello to the World and... hated it. Yes, I absolutely despised the idea of computer programming and wanted to stay as far as I could from it. But as I learned more and more Java and broadened my knowledge of programming as a whole, I knew there was nothing I'd rather do. Since then, I've been certified in Java, gained an understanding of Python, and have recently dived into the world of web development with React.js and HTML/CSS. I'm brimming with excitement and unwavering passion for the boundless possibilities that coding opens up, and I'm immensely grateful for the opportunity to pursue them for the rest of my life.
